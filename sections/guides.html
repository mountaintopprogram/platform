Become a Mountaintop Guide

Mountaintop Guides are role models for humanity - leaders in their communities, scholars, artists, musicians, engineers, entrepreneurs, and worldchangers. We invite Guides to NYC-area high schools to speak openly about both the rewards and trials of their experience, inspiring students to pursue their dreams while building an understanding of what those dreams will take. Our program is flexible and we're able to accommodate arrangements from a single talk to an entire semester collaborating with students. Join us and help inspire the next generation of leaders.

Meet Our Guides!

  • Miles Crettien

    Founder of Verticulture Farms

    Miles is a graduate of Bard College's MBA in Sustainability. There he was trained in innovative project management and sustainable business methodologies which he has used to consult prestigious institutions including UBS and Northeastern University as well as other design/manufacturing startups based in NYC. He received his BA in Environmental Science with a minor in Business Administration from Ithaca College. There, he focused his research on the mechanisms behind plant defenses and biofuel production and lead a project to build a biodiesel processing facility at an eco-reserve in the heart of the Ecuadorian Cloud Rainforest. Additionally, Miles worked six years as an organic vegetable grower on farms throughout Massachusetts and New York. Using his experience in business, agriculture and horticultural research, Miles chose to focus his early career on the development of Urban Agriculture in NYC. He has lead numerous initiatives to promote Urban Agriculture, including co-founding his urban aquaponics start-up, Verticulture Farms LLC, and developing an Urban Farming AGcelerator for Urban Farmer entrepreneurs in NYC.

  • James McArthur

    Entrepreneur and Former MakerBot Intrapreneur

    James McArthur is an entrepreneur who is dedicated to making entrepreneurship accessible and possible for everyone. He is on a mission to change the way the founding process works for new ventures, and he is working to build the systems needed to make sure founders have the tools and support they need to be successful. His background in data modeling, sales and marketing help him ground businesses on strong foundations while building sustainable high-growth ventures. He is a frequent speaker on entrepreneurial strategy and is the founder of Hobcaw Cafe, Basal Consultancy, a former intrepreneur at MakerBot and currently works in sales at Stack Overflow as he builds the next adventure.

  • William Reinisch

    Venture Partner, Paladin Capital

    William Reinisch is a Venture Partner with Paladin Capital Group, a Venture Capital firm, where he is responsible for sourcing and managing venture investments and runs the firm's New York office. He is currently the Chairman of the Entrepreneurship department at Fashion Institute of Technology and also the CEO and Founder of XIV River. Reinisch is also on faculty at Columbia University, New York University, Fashion Institute of Technology and Stevens Institute of Technology. He was previously the Vice President of Strategy and Technology with Motorola. Before that, he was Vice President of Strategy and New Initiatives at Motorola as well as the strategic advisor to the Chief Strategy Office. Prior to Motorola he was the Senior Vice President of operations and engineering at Magnolia Broadband. He also led the global technology organization at Multilink Technology Corporation. He has authored several publications, presentations, US patents and patent applications. He earned an MBA from Fordham University and a B.S. from Rochester Institute of Technology (R.I.T.).

  • Tarek Pertew

    Founding Advisor

    Tarek Pertew is the co-founder of Uncubed, a platform that connects people with high-growth digital careers through daily content, large-scale conferences and new economy skills classes. Tarek is also co-founder of NYC Footy, an adult soccer league favored by many tech companies. Earlier, Tarek founded MyWorkster, a job fair platform for college alumni. Tarek is an advisor to the United Kingdom Trade & Investment Institute (UKTI) and his alma mater, The University of Virginia.

  • Tiffany Kwan

    Strategist

    Tiffany Kwan is on a mission to foster creativity and passion in others. As an associate consultant at Acceleration Group, she uses her combination of analytical and design skills to help innovative ventures and nonprofits transform their operations and launch new solutions that expand their access to opportunity. In her undergraduate studies, despite completing three majors in Manufacturing Management, Production Management, and Entrepreneurship, she was left discovering how her education fit into her ambitions. During her last year in college, she picked up web development in order to create a platform that helps reduce the financial burden of college by promoting the recycling of used textbooks from student communities to the subsequent class. This project became a springboard for her mission and she has developed a deep commitment to enabling young people to discover their entrepreneurial potential and gain the skills to use it.
