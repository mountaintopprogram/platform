The Climb

Mountaintop is a new educational initiative that dares students to find and follow their passions. We source outstanding innovators whose creativity and persistence have improved all of our lives, who we call Mountaintop Guides, then bring them to NYC-area high schools for candid talks about their climb, success, and the pitfalls they encountered along the way. Students come away inspired and energized, and knowing that they too have the potential to change the world.

Participating schools also have the option to pursue a more intensive workshop program, where Guides act as mentors as students pursue their own projects, creating the future of humanity without ever leaving the classroom.
